Precipitation (mm)
  • 0 mm: No rainfall expected.
  • 1-5 mm: Light rain - gentle watering, slightly moistens soil.
  • 5-10 mm: Moderate rain - adequate moisture for crops.
  • 10-20 mm: Heavy rain - noticeable wet conditions, check drainage.
  • 20+ mm: Very heavy rain - potential flooding risk, secure equipment.

Weather & Farming Insights

Precipitation vs Rain Chance

Precipitation shows how much rain will fall (mm). Rain Chance shows the probability it will rain (%).

Example Scenarios

20% chance & 0mm = Cloudy but dry. 80% chance & 5mm = Light rain likely. 90% chance & 30mm = Heavy rain expected.
