Precipitation (mm)
  • 0 mm: No rainfall expected.
  • 1-5 mm: Light rain - gentle watering, slightly moistens soil.
  • 5-10 mm: Moderate rain - adequate moisture for crops.
  • 10-20 mm: Heavy rain - noticeable wet conditions, check drainage.
  • 20+ mm: Very heavy rain - potential flooding risk, secure equipment.
Rain Probability (%)
  • 0-20%: Very unlikely to rain - proceed with outdoor plans.
  • 30-50%: Possible scattered showers - have backup plans ready.
  • 60-80%: Likely rain - bring umbrella ‚òÇÔ∏è and secure crops.
  • 90-100%: Almost certain rain - postpone fieldwork, prepare for storms.
Wind Speed (Km/H)
  • 0-10 Km/H: Calm/Light winds - safe for all agricultural activities.
  • 10-20 Km/H: Moderate winds - suitable for most field operations.
  • 20-40 Km/H: Strong winds - crops may be stressed, secure structures.
  • 40+ Km/H: Very strong winds/storms - do not work outdoors, protect equipment.

Key Concepts

üìä Precipitation vs Rain Chance: Precipitation shows how much rain will fall (mm). Rain Chance shows the probability it will rain (%).

üí° Example Scenarios: 20% chance & 0mm = Cloudy but dry. 80% chance & 5mm = Light rain likely. 90% chance & 30mm = Heavy rain expected.
